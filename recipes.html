Avocado Toast

Ingredients: 1 ripe avocado, 2 slices of bread, salt, pepper, red pepper flakes (optional), lemon juice (optional).

Instructions:

  1. Toast the bread slices until golden brown.
  2. Cut the avocado in half, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh into a bowl.
  3. Mash the avocado with a fork until smooth, adding a squeeze of lemon juice if desired.
  4. Spread the mashed avocado evenly on the toasted bread.
  5. Sprinkle with salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes, then serve.

Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Ingredients: 200g spaghetti, 4 cloves garlic, 1/4 cup olive oil, red pepper flakes, salt, parsley (optional).

Instructions:

  1. Cook the spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente, then drain.
  2. Thinly slice the garlic cloves.
  3. In a large pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat and sauté the garlic until golden.
  4. Add red pepper flakes and cooked spaghetti to the pan, tossing to coat.
    Garnish with chopped parsley if desired, then serve.

Caprese Salad

Ingredients: 2 tomatoes, 1 ball fresh mozzarella, fresh basil leaves, balsamic vinegar, olive oil, salt, pepper.

Instructions:

  1. Slice the tomatoes and mozzarella into even rounds.
  2. Arrange the tomato and mozzarella slices on a plate, alternating each slice.
  3. Tuck fresh basil leaves between the tomato and mozzarella slices.
  4. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic vinegar.
  5. Season with salt and pepper, then serve.

Banana Pancakes

Ingredients: 1 ripe banana, 2 eggs, 1/4 tsp cinnamon, 1/4 tsp vanilla extract, butter or oil for cooking.

Instructions:

  1. Mash the banana in a bowl until smooth.
  2. Whisk in the eggs, cinnamon, and vanilla extract until combined.
  3. Heat butter or oil in a pan over medium heat.
  4. Pour small amounts of batter into the pan to form pancakes.
  5. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until golden brown, then serve.

Egg Fried Rice

Ingredients: 2 cups cooked rice, 2 eggs, 1/2 cup frozen peas and carrots, soy sauce, green onions (optional).

Instructions:

  1. Scramble the eggs in a hot pan with a bit of oil, then set aside.
  2. In the same pan, sauté the frozen peas and carrots until heated through.
  3. Add the cooked rice to the pan and stir to combine with the vegetables.
  4. Return the scrambled eggs to the pan and mix everything together.
  5. Drizzle with soy sauce and garnish with chopped green onions if desired, then serve.

Tomato Basil Bruschetta

Ingredients: 4-5 tomatoes, fresh basil leaves, 1-2 cloves garlic, olive oil, baguette, salt, pepper.

Instructions:

  1. Dice the tomatoes and chop the basil leaves.
  2. Mince the garlic and mix it with the tomatoes and basil in a bowl.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil, and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Slice the baguette and toast the slices until golden brown.
  5. Spoon the tomato mixture onto the toasted baguette slices, then serve.
